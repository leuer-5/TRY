TechNova's New Phone

Introducing the newly launched by TechNova.

The ROG Phone 3 boasts the latest Qualcomm Snapdragon processor that can handle any game you throw at it. It is also paired with 16 GB of RAM and 1 TB of storage. It also has a 144hz screen refresh rate to better satisfy your gaming experience. It is also equipped with a 6000 mAH battery. Side charging design, dual front-facing speakers, and an aero active cooler accessory.

Qualcomm Snapdragon 865+

Powered by the fastest chipset, Qualcomm Snapdragon 865+, together with LPDDR5 RAM and UFS 3.1 ROM, ROG Phone 3 delivers the ultimate gaming experience a person could get.

GameCool 3

The ROG Phone 3 uses advanced 3D vapor chamber and a large heat sink that are precisely placed to eliminate hotspots. The cooling system is incredibly effective at removing heat during heavy gaming sessions. Paired with the aeroactive cooler, you can game endlessly without the anxiety that the phone will throttle its performance.

Battery

ROG Phone 3 boasts a whopping 6000mAH battery to keep you going even on the longest battles. There are multiple battery modes that can significantly prolong battery life.
